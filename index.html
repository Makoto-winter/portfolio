My Service

I have created a service https://www.favteachers.net to send an email whenever my favorite English tutors have more availability. I myself am an English learner and I take one one-to-one online class a day on a platform called Engoo.com. I have struggled to make a reservation for my favorite tutors' class. So, I decided to to create this service. The source code can be found at https://github.com/Makoto-winter/EngooScraper

Email Notification

The service checks the availability of English tutors every 10 minuts and send an email when they have more availability compared to 10 minutes ago.

Scraping

The service scrapes tutors availability every 10 minuts.

Database

The number of available slots is stored in MongoDB.

Comparison

The service compares the number of current number of slots with that of 10 minutes ago.

Bootstrap

It has a nicely formatted one-page landing page created with Bootstrap.

Users

The service can accomodate multiple users. They use their email address to log in.
